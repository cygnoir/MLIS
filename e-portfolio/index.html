Hello! My name is Halsted Bernard and I am a student in Wayne State University's School of Library and Information Science.

In addition to my MLIS degree, I am earning my graduate certificate in Information Management, specializing in web design and development. I am particularly interested in designing and creating digital archives. My advisor is Dr. Joan E. Beaudoin.

On March 1st, I became the Interim Vice-President of SOLIS for the 2011-2012 year. My focus is implementing tools and procedures that will unite both online and traditional cohorts into one engaged and informed community.
